    Welcome to Tigris: Preserving Nature's Majesty

    At Tigris, we invite you to immerse yourself in the unparalleled beauty and biodiversity of nature. Our sanctuary serves as a vital refuge for majestic tigers and a myriad of other wildlife species. Through conservation efforts and sustainable practices, we are dedicated to preserving this precious ecosystem for generations to come.

    • WWF 2024 Award

      In 2024, Tigris Tiger Reserve proudly received the prestigious WWF award for its exceptional dedication to wildlife conservation. This recognition underscores our relentless commitment to protecting endangered species and their habitats.

    • 180 Tigers saved

      Tigris Tiger Reserve's relentless efforts have led to the remarkable rescue and safeguarding of 180 tigers, a testament to our unwavering commitment to preserving these magnificent creatures and their natural habitats.

    • 2M+ Visitors

      With over 2 million visitors, Tigris Tiger Reserve offers an immersive experience in nature's splendor. Our sanctuary's popularity underscores the profound appreciation and support for wildlife conservation efforts within our community and beyond.
